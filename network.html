Software Defined WAN (SD-WAN)

Harbour1 with its underlying Software Defined infrastructure has integrated SD -WAN to its architecture. It has enabled network admins to use bandwidth more efficiently, ensuring high performance for critical applications without compromising on security or data privacy. The unified WAN backbone delivers comprehensive analytics across the enterprise backbone.

Multiprotocol Label Switching (MPLS)

MPLS on Harbour1 enables enterprises to build advanced and intelligent networks that deliver a wide variety of services over a single infrastructure. This solution can be utilized for bandwidth adjustment, to reduce network congestion and increase quality of service. Enterprises with diverse access links can be added on an MPLS edge without changing their current environments.

Direct Connect (P2P)

Seamless and secure Point to Point ethernet connection ensures your premise is directly connected to Harbour1. Enterprises can also establish private connectivity between Harbour1 and their multi cloud environments. This ensures increased bandwidth throughput up to hundreds of Gigs to meet real-time spikes and eases the transfer of large data sets over the wire

Virtual Private Network(VPN)

VPN on Harbour1 provides anonymity by creating a private network over open internet. It enables enterprises to access cloud workloads from anywhere, any time through any device without compromising security. Masking of internet protocol (IP) address keeps your transactions at bay from intrusion. Network admins can use VPN to gain visibility into network, certificate pinning, and encrypting data

Wifi on Cloud

Wi-Fi on Harbour1 allows enterprises to manage the entire network from cloud. It eliminates the need of having the wireless controller hardware on premise thereby ensuring swift scalability. It also service offers users an enhanced experience through location-based and context-aware applications while reducing capex and operational expense with a pay-as-you-grow model.


Content Delivery Network (CDN)

By geographically distributing a network of proxy servers and caching data spatially relative to end users, Harbour1 CDN service ensures high availability and performance. It can be used for content storage and delivery service for web acceleration, file download optimization, transcoding, video on demand and live streaming. Enterprise has the flexibility to choose content across multiple locations across the globe.

Load balancer (LB)

Enterprises can distribute workloads and computing resources within a cloud environment using Harbour1. This process is adopted by organizations and enterprises to manage workload demands by providing resources to multiple systems or servers. Its advantages over conventional load balancing of on-premises assets are lower cost and the simplicity of scaling the application up or down as per business demands.
